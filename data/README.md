# data

Place data file(s) in this folder. Add the dimensions (rows and columns).

### Ahallucination_renamed_variables

- `case_name`: The official title of the legal case.
- `court`: The court where the case was heard.
- `state`: The state(s) or jurisdiction(s) where the case took place.
- `date`: The date when the decision or event related to AI hallucination occurred.
- `parties`: The party responsible for using AI-generated or hallucinated content.
- `ai_tool`: The AI system or model involved in producing hallucinated content.
- `hallucination`: The nature or type of AI hallucination observed.
- `outcome`: The court’s decision or resulting action.
- `monetary_penalty`: Any financial fine or penalty imposed as a result of the AI hallucination.
- `professional_sanction`: Indicates whether professional disciplinary action was taken against the party.
- `key_principle`: The legal or ethical principle highlighted in the case.
- `pointer`: Reference or tag connecting the case to a commentary or analysis.
- `source`: The source link or reference to the full legal decision, article, or document.
- `details`: Additional contextual information or editor’s note summarizing the case background.
